<div class="mp-frame-header" style="background: #339966;">Welcome!</div>
<div class="mp-frame-body">Wikidata is a free and open knowledge base that can be read and edited by both humans and machines. 
<p>Wikidata acts as central storage for the <b>structured data</b> of its Wikimedia sister projects including Wikipedia, Wikivoyage, Wiktionary, Wikisource, and others. 
</p>
Wikidata also provides support to many other sites and services beyond just Wikimedia projects! The content of Wikidata is <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/">available under a free license</a>, <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wikidata:Database_download" title="Special:MyLanguage/Wikidata:Database download">exported using standard formats</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wikidata:Data_access#How_can_I_get_data_out_of_Wikidata?" title="Special:MyLanguage/Wikidata:Data access">can be interlinked to other open data sets</a> on the linked data web.</div></div>

<div class="floatright"><a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wikidata:WikiProject_COVID-19" title="Special:MyLanguage/Wikidata:WikiProject COVID-19"><img alt="Wikiproject COVID-19 - logo.svg" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/80/Wikiproject_COVID-19_-_logo.svg/150px-Wikiproject_COVID-19_-_logo.svg.png" decoding="async" width="150" height="158" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/80/Wikiproject_COVID-19_-_logo.svg/225px-Wikiproject_COVID-19_-_logo.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/80/Wikiproject_COVID-19_-_logo.svg/300px-Wikiproject_COVID-19_-_logo.svg.png 2x" data-file-width="135" data-file-height="142"/></a></div>
<p>As the world combats the ongoing <a href="/wiki/Q81068910" title="Q81068910">COVID-19 pandemic</a>,
Wikidata has been extremely useful in collecting and modelling useful information related to <a href="/wiki/Q84263196" title="Q84263196">COVID-19</a>, from relevant research articles and clinical trials, to epidemiological statistics of various regions, to lexemes for words and phrases commonly associated with the pandemic, and much more. A lot of work from <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wikidata:WikiProject_COVID-19" title="Special:MyLanguage/Wikidata:WikiProject COVID-19">WikiProject COVID-19</a> goes into creating and maintaining these items, bolstering Wikidata's role as a helpful open resource in the pandemic.
</p><p><b>More:</b>
</p>
<ul><li>Check out <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wikidata:Tools" title="Special:MyLanguage/Wikidata:Tools">Wikidata:Tools</a> for some of our best tools and gadgets for using and exploring Wikidata.</li></ul>
Know of an interesting project or research conducted using Wikidata? You can nominate content to be featured on the Main page <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wikidata:WikiProject_Interesting_Content" title="Special:MyLanguage/Wikidata:WikiProject Interesting Content">here</a>!</div></div>
